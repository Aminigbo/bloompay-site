About Us

We have got one goal in mind: To simplify payment for businesses in Africa

HabariPay is on a mission to provide smart, secure, and seamless payment solutions for everyone. With a customer-first approach, we are creating technologies to bring out the best in your business

Who We Are

We are a group of passionate individuals who have one goal in common – to make every payment in Africa digital. To achieve this, we created solutions that enable businesses of any scale to accept and reconcile payments, power their commerce and drive sales. Today, we are helping thousands of businesses simplify their payments and expand their horizons.
